How the portal works

1. Your pricelist

Open My Pricelist to see your agreed prices. Prices update automatically when new lists take effect, so what you see is always current.

2. Placing an order

Click New Order, pick your products and quantities, and submit. You receive an order number immediately. Our team confirms the order before it enters fulfilment. You may attach your own LPO to the order.

3. Tracking orders

The Home page lists your draft, current, and past orders with live status: confirmed, in fulfilment, dispatched, delivered. Open any order to see the detail or download the order PDF.

4. Messages

Questions or changes on an order go through Messages. Our team replies in the portal and you see a notification badge when there is an answer.

5. Offers and promotions

Offers we issue to you appear on your Home page. Review and accept them online; accepted offers convert into orders. Running promotions also show on the Home page.

6. Your account

Change your password any time under Account. If your login serves several outlets, use the outlet selector in the top bar to switch which outlet you are ordering for.

Need help?

Contact your sales representative, or send us a message in the portal. New here? Replay the quick tour.
